Industrial OT Assessor - iOTa

iOTa simplifies the process of conducting cybersecurity focused risk assessments for Industrial Automation and Control Systems (IACS), Industrial Control Systems (ICS), and Operational Technology (OT).
With iOTa, you can conduct multi-scenario CyberPHA/ICS Cybersecurity Risk Assessments utilizing an extensive database of scenarios covering every industry sector.
The software utilizes machine learning and artificial intelligence to provide qualified and quantifiable risk assessments and ratings focused on safety and environmental factors and based on location, industry and facility.
iOTA includes
  • Analysis of multiple scenarios
  • Focus on safety, danger-to-life, and environmental risk factors
  • "Quick" risk assessment worksheet
  • Comprehensive industrial Site walkdown checklist
  • Comprehensive database of scenarios and consequences
  • Automated risk analysis

iOTa is backed with years of hands-on experience across multiple industry sectors. It has been designed to facilitate, simplify, and lower the costs of conducting cybersecurity risk assessments within complex and high risk environments.
