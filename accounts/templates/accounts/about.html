Simplifying Risk

Designed by and for OT Cybersecurity Professionals

We are a team of OT Cybersecurity experts with years of hands-on experience of conducting risk assessments across every conceivable environment. We've worked within industries as diverse as food manufacturing and energy, semiconductors and auto. We know that OT risks require careful consideration across multiple scenarios and that leaders want quantifiable information to help in decision making. We've developed iOTa with these requirements in mind.

Meet Stuart King, iOTa's creator, designer, engineer, and leader

Stuart King has designed iOTa from the ground-up to meet a need that he was seeing at many organizations: namely a means to conduct OT-specific cybersecurity assessments, and generate simple and meaningful reports. Stuart's career spans three decades of hands-on practice across a multitude of industry sectors ranging from manufacturing to energy, financial services to data services.

Stuart has worked for some of the largest and best known organizations in the world including Deloitte, Wipro, Sony, and Tata. He was one of the founding members of the Chartered Institute of Information Security Professionals in the UK (member #7), holds an MBA in Technology Management, and is GICSP certified.
