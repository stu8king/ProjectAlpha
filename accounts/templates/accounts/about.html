Meet Stuart King, iOTa's creator, designer, engineer, and leader

Stuart King has designed iOTa from the ground-up to meet a need that he was seeing at many organizations: namely a means to conduct cybersecurity assessments, focusing on the issues most important to every OT environment: namely safety, the environment, and operational resilience.

Stuart's career spans three decades of hands-on cybersecurity across a multitude of industry sectors ranging from manufacturing to energy, financial services to data services. Over the years, Stuart has completed hundreds of risk assessments, along the way developing a multitude of tools and techniques that provided useful background when building iOTa.

The idea for iOTa came out of seeing organizations being frustrated by the lack of good risk assessment tools and the high cost of buying in the consulting services to conduct OT assessments such as the CyberPHA. The objective was to create a product that allows organizations to simulate and quantify risk scenarios, and simplify reporting and risk tracking

Stuart has worked for some of the largest and best known organizations in the world including Deloitte, Wipro, Sony, and Tata. He was one of the founding members of the Chartered Institute of Information Security Professionals in the UK (member #7), holds an MBA in Technology Management, and is GICSP certified.
