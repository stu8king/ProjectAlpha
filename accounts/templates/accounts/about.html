Stuart King

iOTa Chief Architect, Designer, Software Engineer, and Leader

Stuart King is a dedicated cybersecurity professional who has spent most of the past 25 years helping organizations around the world across many different industry sectors manage and mitigate IT and OT cybersecurity risks.

Stuart's experience has taken him from environments such as oil refineries in the Middle East to Electronics Factories in Japan. Along the way, building an empathy and understanding of the reasons why managing risk for OT is so significantly different from the world of Enterprise IT risk.

Stuart's career begin in the Royal Air Force, learning the programming and networking skills that would all prove to be so useful later on. Along the way, Stuart has been widely published, spoken at many conferences, and was one of the first members of the Chartered Institute of Information Security Professionals
