Privacy Notice

Last Updated: March 24, 2024

This Privacy Notice describes how we collect and use your personal information in relation to the AnzenOT application.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect your personal information in the course of providing and operating AnzenOT.

Here are the types of information we gather:

  • Information You Give Us: We collect any information you provide in relation to your use of the AnzenOT application.
  • Automatic Information: We automatically collect certain types of information when you interact with AnzenOT.
    1. network and connection information, such as the Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer or other device to the Internet and information about your Internet service provider;
    2. computer and device information, such as device, application, or browser type and version, browser plug-in type and version, operating system, or time zone setting;
    3. email addresses and phone numbers used to contact us;

Use of Personal Information

We do not share your information with any third parties. Your information is only used for the purpose of providing the AnzenOT application to you.

How We Secure Information

AnzenOT is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) and takes advantage of many of the security features offered by AWS. ALL access to AnzenOT services is secured with two-factor authentication. Backend databases are encrypted and all access to AnzenOT is logged.
