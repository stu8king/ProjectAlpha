Cybersecurity Risk Management for OT

industrial OT assessor - iOTa

Save time and money with dedicated risk assessment tools, integrated with AI and aligned with industry best practices and standards from around the world. Includes multi-scenario Cybersecurity Process and Hazards Assessment (PHA) and a multi-scenario risk assessment worksheet. Complete focus on environmental health and safety, with quantifiable event costs and risk ratings produced from real-world machine learning algorithms.

CyberPHA

Multi-scenario process and hazards assessment with AI facilitated qualitative and quantitative risk analysis. Automated generation of mitigation measures aligned with selectable standards, and automated reporting.

QRAW

Conduct rapid risk assessments against multiple scenarios facilitated with a built-in scenario-database and an AI facilitated risk analysis. Attach selected scenarios to a risk register and track progress over time with snapshots

Business Ready

Generate PHA profiles that include a safety, chemical, physical security, compliance, and OT system summaries for any given industrial facility. Perform control assessments using the Mitre ATT&CK framework for ICS.

Built by and for OT Cybersecurity Professionals

Enhance your OT risk assessments
and simplify risk analysis

iOTa has been designed to save time and money in performing complex safety and operational focused risk analysis.

Organizations that use iOTa benefit from being able to complete, manage, and track OT risk assessments with minimal or no external assistance and guidance.

Complex scenario analysis is performed in seconds by powerful proprietary AI-integrated processes that present data that is ready to be used by an organization's risk management leaders.

Recommended controls are automatically mapped to any common industry or regulatory standard.

Designed and Developed by Security Professionals

iOTa is developed using an industry standard framework, delivered as a SaaS solution, and securely hosted on AWS.

Secure

Access to iOTa uses two-factor authentication. All access is logged and suspicious attempts to login are blocked.

AI Integration

iOTa has proprietary algorithms that utilize the latest AI integrations to enhance risk assessment, with a specific focus on OT and Industrial Control Systems.

Safety First

iOTa risk assessment focus on the principle that safety is the first and foremost priority for industrial facilities. Assessments are based on the PHA process and use much of the same terminology.
